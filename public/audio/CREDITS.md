# Music credits — game3 Sunrise Farm

Both tracks were published on **FreePD.com**, which dedicated its entire catalog to the
public domain: the site's banner read "Public Domain Music — Creative Commons 0 —
Completely Royalty Free", and each track page stated "This music is available for
commercial and non-commercial purposes."

FreePD.com shut down in late 2025, so the files were retrieved from the Internet
Archive (mirror item `allfreepdmusicbykuronekony4n`), and the license + author facts
were verified against the Wayback Machine snapshot of FreePD's Upbeat page
(web.archive.org/web/20250106221458/https://freepd.com/upbeat.php) on 2026-06-10.

| File | Title | Author | License | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `music/still-pickin.mp3` | Still Pickin | Kevin MacLeod | Public Domain / CC0 (FreePD) | "Good old fashioned pickin and stompin!" — banjo folk, 4:58 |
| `music/happy-whistling-ukulele.mp3` | Happy Whistling Ukulele | Rafael Krux | Public Domain / CC0 (FreePD) | "Happy, positive and cheerful track featuring ukulele, light percussion" — 2:03 |

Files transcoded from the archived 320kbps originals to 128kbps MP3 (metadata
stripped) to keep the bundle light. No attribution is required by the license;
credited here out of courtesy.

All sound *effects* in the game are synthesized at runtime via WebAudio (see
`src/audio/sfx.ts`) — no sample files.
